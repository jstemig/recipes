450 oven 



2 c flour

1 T sugar

1 T baking powder

1 t salt

1/2 c cold butter, cut in T

3/4 c buttermilk ( or milk + 1 t lemon juice, for 5 mins)


Mix flour, sugar, baking poweder, salt

Cut in butter till pea size

Add buttermilk, make a ball

Roll into 6" x 9"; cut into thirds

Stack, roll again into 6" x 9" 

Cut

Bake 10-12
