4 bacon slices
1/4 lb good quality loose Mexican chorizo
1/2 white onion, chopped
1-2 serrano chiles
2 garlic cloves, minced
1 t mexican oregano
1/2 red bell, chopped
1 c chopped whole peeled tomatoes
4-6 c cooked beans (pinto or mayocoba)
1/2 c dark lager (think negra modelo)
stock or water as needed

Cook bacon till rendered
Remove bacon

Cook chorizo, 10 minutes

Add onion & chiles, cook till soft, 5-7 mins

Add garlic, oregano, bell pepper, tomatoes, cook 2-3 mins

Add beans, beer & water, going for a soupy consistency.

Add salt & pepper, simmer 20 mins.

Serve w/ onion, cilantro, bacon, limes
