Simmer beans till tender 

Fry together:
3 t oil or ghee
1 t cumin
1 t black mustard seed

Then add 
4-5 garlic chopped, fry till golden

Then add
1/2 onion chopped 
fry till translucent

Then add 
1 c tomato chopped
1 t fresh ginger grated
1/2 t green chile
fry till soft

Then add
1/2 t cayenne
1/4 t turmeric
1/2 t garam masala
1 t coriander

Cook till oil leaves sides

Then add
1 t fenugreek leaves

Add Beans, stir, simmer for 10 minutes
