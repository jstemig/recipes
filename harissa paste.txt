4 dried New Mexico chiles, stemmed, seeded
1/2 c veg oil
6 cloves garlic
1 t caraway
1 t cumin
1 c drained roasted red peppers 
1/2 c sun dried tomatoes 
1 T white balsamic 


Cook chilis, oil, garlic, caraway and cumin till garlic is golden.  

Process with remaining ingredients until smooth.  

Salt and cayenne to taste.
